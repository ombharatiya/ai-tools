# AI tools and apps for a Writer and Content Creator

For people who produce writing and media for an audience or client: articles, copy, scripts, newsletters, video, and audio. AI is strong at drafts and editing. It is weak at facts, so you stay the editor.

## Who this is for

Journalists, copywriters, bloggers, novelists, newsletter writers, and multimedia creators, whether freelance or in-house.

## Starter stack

1. [ChatGPT](../tools/ai-assistants/chatgpt.md) or [Claude](../tools/ai-assistants/claude.md) (freemium): your main drafting and ideation tool. Claude is often preferred for longer, natural prose.
2. **Grammarly** (freemium, review pending): everyday editing, clarity, and tone.
3. **CapCut** or **Descript** (freemium, review pending): if you make video or audio.
4. **ElevenLabs** (freemium, review pending): if you need voiceover.

## Tasks and the tools that help

### Drafting, outlining, and brainstorming

- [ChatGPT](../tools/ai-assistants/chatgpt.md) (freemium): strong general drafting and ideation.
- [Claude](../tools/ai-assistants/claude.md) (freemium): favored for longer prose that follows a brief.
- [Gemini](../tools/ai-assistants/gemini.md) (freemium): drafting with large context and Google integration.

### Editing, grammar, clarity, and tone

- **Grammarly** (freemium, review pending): grammar, clarity, and rewrite suggestions across your apps.
- **ProWritingAid** (freemium, review pending): deep style and readability reports, popular with authors.
- [Claude](../tools/ai-assistants/claude.md) or [ChatGPT](../tools/ai-assistants/chatgpt.md) (freemium): flexible line editing on request.

### SEO and content optimization

- **Surfer SEO** (paid, review pending): on-page optimization and content scoring against search results.
- **Clearscope** (paid, review pending): content optimization for SEO teams.
- **Jasper** (paid, review pending): marketing-focused generation with brand voice.

### Repurposing one piece into many

- **Copy.ai** (freemium, review pending): marketing copy and repurposing workflows.
- [ChatGPT](../tools/ai-assistants/chatgpt.md) or [Claude](../tools/ai-assistants/claude.md) (freemium): do this well with a good prompt at lower cost.

### Fiction and narrative writing

- **Sudowrite** (paid, review pending): built for novelists, for brainstorming, expanding, and rewriting scenes.
- **NovelCrafter** (paid, review pending): organizes a novel project and connects to the model of your choice.

### Video, voice, and audio

- **Descript** (freemium, review pending): edit video and audio by editing the transcript.
- **CapCut** (freemium, review pending): fast short-form video editing with AI captions. Owned by ByteDance.
- **ElevenLabs** (freemium, review pending): text to speech and voice cloning for narration.
- For images: [Midjourney](../tools/image-generation/midjourney.md), [OpenAI image generation](../tools/image-generation/dall-e.md), or [Stable Diffusion](../tools/image-generation/stable-diffusion.md).

## Cautions for this profession

- **Hallucinated facts and citations.** Chatbots confidently invent statistics, sources, and quotes. Fact-check everything. Never trust an AI-supplied citation without finding the original.
- **Privacy and retention.** Consumer tiers may use your inputs for training and can be subject to legal holds (a US court ordered OpenAI to preserve ChatGPT logs in the New York Times case, treated differently for Enterprise). Do not paste client confidential or embargoed material into consumer tiers. Turn off training where you can.
- **Copyright of AI output.** In the US, purely AI-generated text and images are not protected by copyright. Heavily AI-written work may not be yours to own or license exclusively. Your own edits and arrangement can be protectable.
- **Plagiarism and overlap.** Generated text can echo existing writing. Run a plagiarism check for client or published work.
- **Disclosure and detection.** Many publishers, schools, and clients restrict undisclosed AI content. Know the rules and disclose when required. AI detectors are unreliable and produce false positives, so do not treat them as proof either way.
- **Voice cloning consent.** Only clone a voice you have the rights to.
- **Brand voice drift.** Unedited AI copy tends toward generic phrasing. Edit for your or the client's voice.

## How to choose

One general assistant plus Grammarly covers most writers. Add an SEO tool only if you write for search traffic, a fiction tool only if you write fiction, and audio or video tools only for those formats. Resist paying for three tools that each do part of the same job.
