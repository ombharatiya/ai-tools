# AI tools and apps for a Sales Professional

For reps who find, qualify, and close prospects through outreach, calls, and follow-up while keeping a CRM current. AI helps with research, drafts, and call notes. The pattern that works is AI for prep, humans for the relationship.

## Who this is for

B2B and B2C sales reps, SDRs and BDRs, account executives, and the RevOps people who support them.

## Starter stack

1. **HubSpot** (freemium, review pending): a genuinely free CRM with AI built in.
2. **Apollo.io** (freemium, review pending): prospecting data plus sequencing in one place.
3. **Lavender** (freemium, review pending): better cold emails through live coaching.
4. **Fathom** or **Fireflies** (freemium, review pending): call notes and summaries.
5. [ChatGPT](../tools/ai-assistants/chatgpt.md) or [Claude](../tools/ai-assistants/claude.md) (freemium): research, drafting, and prep.

## Tasks and the tools that help

### Prospecting and lead research

- **Apollo.io** (freemium, review pending): a large contact database plus sequencing and a dialer.
- **ZoomInfo** (enterprise, review pending): enterprise B2B data with deep firmographics.
- **Clay** (freemium, review pending): no-code enrichment that runs lookups across many providers, plus AI research.

### Writing outreach

- **Lavender** (freemium, review pending): real-time email coaching and scoring as you write.
- **Outreach** or **Salesloft** (paid, review pending): sequencing platforms with AI drafting built in.
- [ChatGPT](../tools/ai-assistants/chatgpt.md) or [Claude](../tools/ai-assistants/claude.md) (freemium): personalizing at the individual level.

### Call recording and conversation intelligence

- **Gong** (enterprise, review pending): records and analyzes calls for deal risk and coaching, the market leader.
- **Chorus** (enterprise, review pending): conversation intelligence bundled with ZoomInfo.
- **Fireflies**, **Fathom**, or **Otter** (freemium, review pending): more affordable AI notetakers.

### CRM hygiene, forecasting, and deal management

- **HubSpot (Breeze AI)** (freemium, review pending): CRM with built-in AI, free for small teams.
- **Salesforce (Einstein and Agentforce)** (enterprise, review pending): enterprise CRM with AI add-ons and agents.
- **Clari** (enterprise, review pending): revenue and pipeline forecasting.

### Practice, coaching, and onboarding

- **Hyperbound** (paid, review pending): AI buyer roleplay for cold calls and discovery, with scorecards.
- **Nooks** or **Mindtickle** (paid, review pending): roleplay plus broader enablement.

## Cautions for this profession

- **Data accuracy.** Contact databases are often wrong. Email accuracy and phone match rates vary widely. Verify before you dial or send, and expect bounces.
- **Outreach compliance.** Cold email and calling are regulated (CAN-SPAM, GDPR, CCPA, TCPA, Do-Not-Call, and call-recording consent laws that differ by state and country). Two-party-consent states require disclosure that a call is recorded. AI does not make you compliant. You are still responsible.
- **Deliverability.** Mass AI-generated sequences can get your domain flagged. Warm up domains, keep volumes sane, and personalize for real.
- **Hallucination in research.** AI research agents can fabricate company facts, titles, or recent news. Confirm specifics before referencing them with a prospect.
- **Privacy of recordings.** Conversation tools store sensitive customer calls. Know where data lives, who can access it, retention, and whether customers consented.
- **Over-automation.** Buyers can tell when outreach is robotic. Use AI for research and first drafts, humans for judgment and relationship.
- **Cost and lock-in.** Gong, ZoomInfo, Salesforce, and Clari carry large annual commitments and seat minimums. Confirm total cost and contract length.

## How to choose

A new rep can run almost everything on free tiers: HubSpot for the CRM, Apollo for data, a notetaker, and a general assistant. Move up to the enterprise platforms (Gong, ZoomInfo, Salesforce) when a team needs shared forecasting and coaching at scale, and budget for the commitment.
