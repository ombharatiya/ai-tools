# AI tools and apps for a Marketer

For people who plan and run campaigns across content, SEO, social, email, and analytics. AI helps with drafts, optimization, and visuals. The risks are off-brand output, thin content that hurts search, and stacking up tools that overlap.

## Who this is for

Content marketers, digital marketers, social media managers, and growth and email marketers, in-house or at an agency.

## Starter stack

1. [ChatGPT](../tools/ai-assistants/chatgpt.md) or [Claude](../tools/ai-assistants/claude.md) (freemium): drafting, ideation, and repurposing.
2. **Canva** (freemium, review pending): visuals and simple video.
3. **Surfer SEO** (paid, review pending) or, on a budget, **Semrush** or **Ahrefs** trials plus free **GA4**: SEO and content.
4. **Mailchimp** or **Brevo** (freemium, review pending): email campaigns.
5. **Buffer** (freemium, review pending): social scheduling.

## Tasks and the tools that help

### Writing and editing copy

- **Jasper** (paid, review pending): marketing platform with brand-voice training and templates.
- **Copy.ai** (freemium, review pending): copy and go-to-market workflows.
- [ChatGPT](../tools/ai-assistants/chatgpt.md), [Claude](../tools/ai-assistants/claude.md), or [Gemini](../tools/ai-assistants/gemini.md) (freemium): strong general drafting and rewriting.

### SEO research and content optimization

- **Surfer SEO** (paid, review pending): on-page optimization and content briefs.
- **Semrush** (paid, review pending): full SEO, keyword, and competitor platform with AI features.
- **Ahrefs** (paid, review pending): keywords, backlinks, and audits.

### Images and video for campaigns

- **Canva Magic Studio** (freemium, review pending): design plus AI image, text, and video, easy for non-designers.
- **Adobe Firefly** (freemium, review pending): AI images trained on licensed content, marketed as commercially safer.
- [Midjourney](../tools/image-generation/midjourney.md) (paid): high-quality concept and mood imagery.

### Social content and scheduling

- **Buffer** (freemium, review pending): scheduling with an AI assistant for captions, good for small teams.
- **Hootsuite** (paid, review pending): scheduling, analytics, and an AI writer.
- **Later** or **Sprout Social** (paid, review pending): other established schedulers, Sprout being the enterprise-leaning option.

### Email marketing

- **Mailchimp** (freemium, review pending): email with AI content and subject-line help.
- **Klaviyo** (freemium, review pending): email and SMS for ecommerce, strong with Shopify.
- **Brevo** (freemium, review pending): email, SMS, and automation, priced per email sent.

### Research, analytics, and reporting

- **NotebookLM** (free, review pending): grounds answers in your own briefs and reports.
- **Perplexity** (freemium, review pending): cited web research and competitive scans.
- **Google Analytics 4** (free, review pending): the default measurement tool, with a learning curve.

## Cautions for this profession

- **Brand voice and accuracy.** AI drafts drift off-brand and invent facts, stats, and quotes. Treat every output as a first draft and verify any number before publishing.
- **Originality.** AI can reproduce phrasing close to existing content. Run important pieces through editorial review and a plagiarism check.
- **Image copyright.** Generated images can resemble protected work, and commercial-use indemnification varies by tool and plan. Check the license tier before commercial use.
- **AI search risk.** Mass-produced thin AI content can hurt rankings and trust. Search rewards genuinely useful content.
- **Data privacy.** Do not paste customer lists or unreleased campaigns into consumer tiers, which may train on inputs. Business and enterprise tiers generally do not. Confirm your plan.
- **Compliance.** Email must respect CAN-SPAM, GDPR, and similar rules on consent and unsubscribe. Some regions expect disclosure of AI-generated media.
- **Cost creep.** Many of these tools overlap. Avoid paying three times for the same job.

## How to choose

A general assistant plus Canva covers a surprising amount. Add an SEO platform only if organic search matters to you, and one email and one social tool sized to your volume. Pick Firefly over other image tools when commercial safety is the priority.
