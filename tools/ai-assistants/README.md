# General AI assistants

Broad chat assistants you talk to in plain language for writing, analysis, coding, search, and more. These are the all-rounders.

**Last verified:** 2026-06-05

## Quick pick

- **You want the most capable, feature-rich all-rounder:** [ChatGPT](chatgpt.md).
- **You want the best coding help, long-document work, and a no-training-by-default posture:** [Claude](claude.md).
- **You live in Google products (Search, Gmail, Docs, Android):** [Gemini](gemini.md).
- **Privacy is your top concern:** [Claude](claude.md), which does not train on your chats unless you turn it on.

## The honest summary

These three are close in raw capability and all change fast. The clearest way to choose is by what you care about most. ChatGPT has the broadest features (voice, image generation, the largest ecosystem) and the lowest paid entry point, but it trains on personal-tier chats by default and now shows ads on its cheaper tiers in the US. Claude is the pick for coding and long documents and has the best privacy default of the three, since it does not train on your conversations unless you opt in, though accepting that prompt raises retention to five years. Gemini is the natural choice if you already live in Google products and want AI inside Search, Gmail, and Docs, but its default privacy posture is the weakest, with human review of a subset of chats.
