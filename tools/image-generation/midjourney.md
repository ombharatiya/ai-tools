## What it is

Midjourney is a commercial text-to-image (and now image-to-video) generator known for a strong, stylized look out of the box. You write a prompt and it returns images. It started inside Discord and now has its own website with a full editor and gallery. The standing default model is V7, with V8.1 as the newer, faster opt-in model.

## Best for

- Artists, designers, and marketers who want the best-looking results with the least effort.
- Hobbyists comfortable in a web or Discord workflow.
- People who care more about aesthetic quality than control or integration.

## Not for

- Developers who need an API. There is no official one.
- Anyone needing guaranteed privacy on a budget tier.
- People who need to run offline or want reproducible, fine-grained control.

## Pricing

Four monthly tiers, with about 20% off if paid annually. No free tier and no free trial. Last verified 2026-06-05.

<details>
<summary><strong>Full pricing</strong></summary>

- **Basic:** $10/mo (about $8/mo annual). Roughly 3.3 fast GPU hours per month.
- **Standard:** $30/mo (about $24/mo annual). 15 fast GPU hours plus unlimited Relax mode.
- **Pro:** $60/mo (about $48/mo annual). 30 fast GPU hours, Relax, and Stealth mode.
- **Mega:** $120/mo (about $96/mo annual). 60 fast GPU hours, Relax, and Stealth.

The official docs could not be loaded directly during research (they blocked the fetcher), so the GPU-hour figures, especially for Mega, are approximate and worth confirming on the site.

## Strengths

- Best-in-class default aesthetic. Images look polished without heavy prompt engineering.
- Strong coherence for hands, bodies, textures, and lighting in recent models.
- V8.1 produces 2K images directly and renders much faster than older versions.
- A web editor with inpainting, style references, character references, and image-to-video in one product.
- A large community and a public gallery that doubles as prompt inspiration.

## Weaknesses and dealbreakers

- **Dealbreaker for developers:** no official API. Programmatic use means relying on unofficial resellers that can break or violate the terms.
- **Dealbreaker for budget privacy:** images are public by default, and private generation (Stealth) is only on Pro and Mega. In shared Discord channels others can always see your work.
- No free tier and no free trial, so you must pay to try it at all.
- No opt-out of your prompts and images being used to train Midjourney's models.
- Weaker than the best rivals at rendering long, accurate text inside images.
- Less control than a local pipeline. You cannot swap models, weights, or run custom fine-tunes.

## Stability and maturity

Mature and heavily used, but it changes fast, moving through V6, V7, V8, and V8.1 within roughly a year, plus pricing and policy changes. It is funded by its own revenue rather than a large parent, which means independence but no deep-pocketed backer if it stumbles. It has been named in copyright litigation, which is an ongoing legal risk to watch.

## Privacy and data

Public-by-default gallery. Stealth mode (Pro and Mega only) hides your creations on the website but not in shared Discord channels. Prompts and images are used to train Midjourney's models with no opt-out. The company states it does not sell personal data but may share via advertising cookies. Independent privacy reviewers rate its practices poorly.

## Integrations

Runs through the Midjourney website (full editor) and a Discord bot. No official API and no local install. Output can be exported and taken into other editors.

## Licensing of generated images

Paying subscribers own the assets they create, to the fullest extent allowed by law, and ownership persists after downgrade or cancellation. All paid tiers grant commercial use. One catch: if you or your employer earn over $1,000,000 per year in revenue, you must be on Pro or Mega to own your assets. Separately, under current US copyright decisions, purely AI-generated portions may not be copyrightable regardless of what the terms grant. Confirm the current terms before relying on this.

## Alternatives

- **[OpenAI image generation](dall-e.md):** when you need an API, accurate text in images, or precise editing.
- **[Stable Diffusion](stable-diffusion.md):** when you want local, private, customizable generation with no per-image cost.

## Bottom line

**Top pick** for the best default look with the least effort, which is why artists and marketers choose it. The honest tradeoffs are real: no official API, no free tier, weak privacy on cheaper plans, and no opt-out of training. If you need integration, automation, or privacy, look at OpenAI image generation or Stable Diffusion. For pure visual quality with minimal fuss, Midjourney is hard to beat.
